Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Overview

PixelProTools provides browser-based image utilities. We value your privacy. Whenever possible, tools process images in your browser so files are not uploaded to our servers.

What we collect

  • We do not collect images unless a feature explicitly sends files to our server (this site uses client-side processing for most tools).
  • Basic analytics (optional) — you can disable by contacting us.

Third-party services

We may use third-party libraries (jsPDF, Cropper.js, ONNX Runtime) loaded from CDNs. These libraries are subject to their own privacy policies.
